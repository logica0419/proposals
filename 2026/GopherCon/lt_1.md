# From Gophers EX to GopherCon Japan: The Journey of Connecting Japan to the Global Go Community

## Status

### ❔ In Evaluation

## Short Abstract

Many Japanese Gophers dream of engaging with the global Go community, but language barriers often hold them back from taking the leap.  
Through the Gophers EX project, I've spent the past year helping Japanese Gophers overcome those barriers and participate in international conferences as attendees or speakers. Along the way, we realized that true connection works both ways: it isn't just about sending people abroad, it's also about bringing the world to Japan.  
In this lightning talk, I'll share that journey, what we learned from it, and how it eventually led to the launch of GopherCon Japan.
